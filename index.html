Of The Day

Privacy Policy

Last modified: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Of The Day ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Of The Day mobile app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the app and its related features:

  • Account information: Google account data used for sign-in, such as your Google account identifier, name, email address, and profile photo.
  • Gameplay data: Your guesses, attempt counts, puzzle results, challenge history, streak-related progress, and leaderboard performance.
  • Premium purchase data: Product identifiers, entitlement status, purchase timestamps, transaction or provider identifiers, and related purchase records needed to verify premium access.
  • Rewarded ad data: Reward identifiers, ad network details, and placement details needed to validate bonus-attempt claims.
  • Technical and device data: App, device, and request information used for reliability, security, fraud prevention, and troubleshooting.
  • Local storage data: Signed-in session information stored on your device so you can stay logged in.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • sign you in and maintain your account session
  • run the daily game and save your progress
  • show history and leaderboard results
  • validate rewarded ad claims
  • process premium access
  • detect abuse, fraud, duplicate reward claims, and service misuse
  • maintain, secure, and improve the app and backend services
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with service providers that help us operate the app, including:

  • Google, for sign-in and rewarded ad services
  • RevenueCat, for premium purchase and entitlement processing
  • Hosting, database, and infrastructure providers that support app operations

If leaderboard features are enabled, your display name and gameplay results may be visible to other users inside the app.

We may also disclose information when required to comply with applicable law, respond to lawful requests, protect users and the service, or prevent fraud and abuse.

4. Advertising

Of The Day uses Google Mobile Ads for rewarded ad features. Google and related ad services may process device and ad interaction data according to their own policies and settings.

5. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the app, maintain gameplay history and leaderboard records, verify premium access, prevent abuse, resolve disputes, and meet legal obligations.

When information is no longer needed, we delete it or de-identify it where reasonably practical.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us so we can review and remove it where appropriate.

8. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or request a copy of your personal information, or to object to certain processing.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the last modified date and provide additional notice in the app or through the app store listing where appropriate.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests, use the support contact listed in the app store listing or other official publisher contact channel.
